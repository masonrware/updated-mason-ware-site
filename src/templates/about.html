Who is this guy?

Hi everyone, my name is Mason. I am currently an undergraduate student at Brandeis Univeristy studying Computer Science and Applied Mathematics. I became interested in Computer Science because of Game Development, and while I am no longer pursuing such a career, I am still an avid gamer. I grew up in Los Angeles, California as the elder child in a single-parent-household. My fascination with computers started at a very young age. I think it really started with Legos, but the first time I picked up a soldering iron in my middle school STEM class, I knew I had found something I connected with. My official relationship with Computer Science started when I took a CS class in high school. Learning Java and doing problem sets started me down a path that I am still walking. Since my admittance to Brandeis, I have stratified my CS interests, branching into fields like systems programming and learning complimentary languages like Rust and C++.


Currently, I am working to become a black-belt in Python development and bolster my skills in relevant CS areas. Reading programming books, taking CS courses, and gaining internship experience are all ways in which I am striving to do so. My last oppurtunity, at Cleeng, was informative and worthwhile as it strengthened my web-development skills greatly. My next oppurtunity I hope will be in the fields of networking and cloud programming. Overall, getting 'cracked' at python, rust, and C++ is my most realistic next step and I hope to make it the first of many in a comprehensive and successful career.
